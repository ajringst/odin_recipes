Tammy's Chocolate Chip Cookies

Info

  • Makes 6 dozen cookies
  • Takes ~ 1 - 1.5 hours to make all the cookies from start to finish, depending on the size of your oven
  • Peanut free!

Tips from Tammy herself:

  • For better taste and texture use real butter, real vanilla, and old fashioned rolled oats
  • Use a medium or large ice cream scoop for uniform cookies
  • If short-handed for Rice Krispies, Go Lean Crunch can be used as a substitute
  • For non-chocolate lovers, or for a healthier option substitute chocolate chips with raisins or a dried fruit of your choice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp. real vanilla
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • ½ tsp. baking powder
  • ½ tsp. salt
  • 2 cups crisp rice cereal
  • 2 cups large flake rolled oats
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 cup coconut (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Using a mixer, combine butter and sugar together. Once an even texture, beat in eggs one at a time. Add the vanilla.
  3. In a separate bowl combine and stir together flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Once combined, add to the mixer, and mix.
  4. Add cereal, oats, chocolate chips, and coconut to a large bowl. Add in mixed ingredients and stir together using those big muscles of yours. Mix well; dough will be thick. Use medium ice cream scoop to arrange on baking sheet with parchment paper. Flatten cookie dough balls slightly before baking.
  5. Bake in 375* oven for 8-9 minutes until golden. Let cool.
