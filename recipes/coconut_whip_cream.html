Coconut Whipped Cream

Info

  • Vegan!
  • Only two ingredients!
  • A delicious topping to cakes, cupcakes, or a prefect dip for fruit or cookies!

Tips

  • Colder equipment makes for fluffier more stable whip cream. If you can, use a chilled bowl.
  • For best results chill the final product in the fridge for at least 3 hours or overnight.
