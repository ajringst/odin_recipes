Coconut Whipped Cream

Info

  • Vegan!
  • Only two ingredients!
  • A delicious topping to cakes, cupcakes, or a prefect dip for fruit or cookies!

Tips

  • Chill you coconut milk can before making the whip
  • Colder equipment makes for fluffier more stable whip cream. Chill the bowl and whisk attachment of your mixer in the freezer for 10 minutes before whipping.
  • For best results chill the final product in the fridge for at least 3 hours or overnight.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (13.5oz) of chilled coconut milk (not light)
  • &frac23 cup powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Chill bowl and whisk attachment of standing mixer in the freezer for 10 minutes.
  2. Skim the solidified coconut cream from the chilled can of coconut milk and transfer the solids to the bowl. Do not include any coconut water, as it can dramatically change the success of whipping.
  3. Add the powdered sugar to the bowl and whip the contents together in your standing mixer until nice and fluffy.
  4. Let the whip firm up in the fridge for 3 hours or overnight.
  5. Spread on cake, cupcakes, or use as dip for fruit or other sweet treats!
